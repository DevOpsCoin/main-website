Tokenomics (BNB Launch)

$DEVOPS relaunches on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. Distribution is transparent — no presale, no VC allocation, and no hidden minting.

  • 10% — Founder Vesting (3-month cliff, 20-month linear vest)
  • 10% — Treasury (Ops, Buybacks, Marketing)
  • 15% — Community Wallet
  • 5% — Ship-It Fund
  • 10% — Reserve (locked ≥ 12 months via ReserveLock)
  • 40% — Public Circulating Supply (LP + initial float)

Total allocation: 100% (fixed supply, no minting).

Treasury, Fees & Burn

$DEVOPS includes an adjustable transaction-fee system that sustains the ecosystem. Fees are disabled by default and can be toggled as needed.

  • 0.5% → Treasury (operations, marketing, listings)
  • 0.5% → Ship-It Fund (grants for open-source innovation)
  • 0.5% → Burn (permanently reducing supply)

Fees are collected in $DEVOPS (not BNB), capped at 3%, and excluded from owner transfers.
