Principles

$DEVOPS isn’t just using a tech buzzword — the project is structured around real DevOps principles that bring stability, speed, and culture to the memecoin world.

  • 🚀 CI/CD: Frequent, incremental improvements with fast feedback loops.
  • 🤖 Automation First: Manual work is minimized through bots and workflows.
  • 🧠 Infrastructure as Code: Everything is versioned and reproducible.
  • 🌐 Observability & Feedback: Signals are tracked early to stay aligned.
  • 🧰 Continuous Improvement: Release early and often.
  • 🤝 Collaboration & Transparency: Builders, marketers, and meme creators collaborate openly.
  • 🧱 Resilience Through Culture: DevOps principles give structure to chaos.

Tokenomics

$DEVOPS launched on pump.fun with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. All tokens are distributed on-chain through the bonding curve — no presale, no VC allocations, and no hidden minting.

  • 🪙 Fixed Supply: 1 Billion $DEVOPS.
  • 🌐 Launch Platform: pump.fun bonding curve.
  • 🧠 Stealth Builder Phase: Token live, no public promotion yet.
  • 🧑‍💻 Early Builder Participation: Access through awareness.
  • 🚫 No VC / No Team Allocation: No private investors, no lockups.
  • 🔒 Liquidity: Auto-paired and locked at curve graduation.
  • ⛓ Token Standard: SPL Token on Solana.

Wallet Structure & Transparency

$DEVOPS uses a transparent and structured wallet model to support ecosystem growth, community initiatives, and long-term alignment. Each wallet has a clear purpose, and all major addresses are published below for verification.

Wallet Purpose
Founder Vesting Wallet Holds the founder’s personal allocation. This wallet will be locked and vested to align incentives with the community over the long term.
Treasury Wallet Funds strategic initiatives such as LP seeding, marketing, exchange listings, partnerships, and ecosystem development. It also allocates a portion of profits to the Ship It Fund for open-source DevOps grants.
Community Wallet Used for bounties, airdrops, community rewards, and early contributor incentives. All disbursements are traceable on-chain.
Buyback Wallet Holds SOL for open-market $DEVOPS buybacks to support price. Purchased tokens are burned or transferred to the Treasury.
Trading Wallet (Internal) Used for strategic accumulation during the bonding curve phase and potential future liquidity operations. This wallet operates transparently and is controlled by the project.
Trading Wallet (External) Project startup was initially funded through a personal wallet. That wallet will no longer be used for business purposes going forward. All future project funding will be conducted through dedicated business-controlled wallets under the LLC.

Ship It Fund — Open Source DevOps Grants

A portion of Treasury profits is allocated to the Ship It Fund — a community-driven initiative to support fun, useful, or downright chaotic open-source DevOps projects.

  • Microgrants: Funding small DevOps tools, GitHub Actions, meme infrastructure, or other OSS projects.
  • Community Voting: Token holders help decide which projects receive grants.
  • Transparency: All grant transactions are public, and funded projects are showcased on the website.

Devs build the internet — $DEVOPS gives them a rocket.

The Ship It Fund is fully bootstrapped by the founder in the early stages. Initial grants will be modest (e.g. the equivalent of tens of thousands of $DEVOPS tokens) to reward meaningful contributions and test the model. As the project gains traction and Treasury grows, the Ship It Fund’s budget will scale proportionally.
