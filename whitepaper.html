Tokenomics (BNB Launch)

$DEVOPS will relaunch on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. Distribution uses a transparent public launch — no presale, no VC, no hidden allocations.

  • 10% — Founder Vesting (3-month cliff, 20-month linear vest)
  • 10% — Treasury
  • 10% — Community Wallet
  • 5% — Ship-It Fund
  • 5% — Liquidity Pool (locked at launch)
  • 60% — Reserve / Ecosystem

Total allocation: 100%.

The Ship-It Fund

The Ship-It Fund is the heart of $DEVOPS — a community-driven movement that funds fun, useful, and innovative open-source projects across all domains. Whether it's automation, creative tools, or experimental OSS ideas, we reward builders who ship.

  • Receives 5% of the total supply and 0.5% of every taxed transaction when fees are enabled.
  • Funds microgrants and sponsorships for impactful open-source innovation.
  • All awards, votes, and transactions are transparent and verifiable on-chain.

Every transaction helps fund the builders shaping the next wave of open-source creativity.

Treasury, Fees & Burn

$DEVOPS includes an adjustable transaction-fee system that sustains the ecosystem. Fees are disabled by default and can be toggled as needed.

  • 0.5% → Treasury (operations, marketing, listings)
  • 0.5% → Ship-It Fund (grants for open-source innovation)
  • 0.5% → Burn (permanently reducing supply)

Fees are collected in $DEVOPS (not BNB), capped at 3%, and excluded from owner transfers.
