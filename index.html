Wallet Structure

$DEVOPS maintains a transparent, multi-wallet structure: Founder (vesting), Treasury, Community, Ship-It Fund, and Reserve wallets. Wallet addresses will be published once contracts are deployed on BNB Smart Chain.

For complete details, see the whitepaper.

Founder Vesting Wallet

The Founder allocation (~10% of total supply) will be locked upon launch, with a 3-month cliff followed by a 5% monthly linear vesting schedule over 20 months. All vesting transactions will be fully transparent and visible on-chain.

Treasury & Marketing Wallet

Used for LP seeding, marketing, listings, partnerships, and ecosystem growth. Also funds open-source innovation through the Ship-It Fund. All activity will be publicly viewable post-launch for transparency.

Community Distribution Wallet

Used for bounties, airdrops, and rewards for active community members. Details will be shared once live on BNB Smart Chain.

Buyback Wallet

Funded in native BNB. Executes open-market buys of $DEVOPS to support price stability. Purchased tokens may be burned or cycled back into Treasury depending on community vote.
